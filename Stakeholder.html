How to read this map

Each quadrant combines two dimensions: how much power the stakeholder has, and how much they care. Different groups require different communication strategies.

  • Top-right: high power, high interest - manage closely.
  • Top-left: high interest, low power - keep informed.
  • Bottom-right: high power, low interest - keep satisfied.
  • Bottom-left: low power, low interest - minimal contact.
